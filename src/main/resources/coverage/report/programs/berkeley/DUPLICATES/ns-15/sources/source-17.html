21 /**
22  * This exception is thrown by a Replica to indicate it could not meet the
23  * consistency requirements as defined by the
24  * <code>ReplicaConsistencyPolicy</code> in effect for the transaction, within
25  * the allowed timeout period.
26  * <p>
27  * A Replica will typically keep current with its Master. However, network
28  * problems, excessive load on the Master, or Replica, may prevent the Replica
29  * from keeping up and the Replica may fall further behind than is permitted by
30  * its consistency policy. If the Replica cannot catch up in the time defined
31  * by its <code>ReplicaConsistencyPolicy</code>, it will throw this exception
32  * from the {@link com.sleepycat.je.Environment#beginTransaction
33  * Environment.beginTransaction} method, thus preventing the transaction from
34  * accessing data that does not meet its consistency requirements.
35  * <p>
36  * If this exception is encountered frequently, it indicates that the
37  * consistency policy requirements are too strict and cannot be met routinely
38  * given the load being placed on the system and the hardware resources that
39  * are available to service the load. The exception may also indicate that
40  * there is a network related issue that is preventing the Replica from
41  * communicating with the master and keeping up with the replication stream.
42  * <p>
43  * The application can choose to retry the transaction, until the underlying
44  * system problem has been resolved. Or it can try relaxing the consistency
45  * constraints, or choose the {@link NoConsistencyRequiredPolicy} so that the
46  * constraints can be satisfied more easily.
47  * For example, in a {@link <a href=
48  * "{@docRoot}/../ReplicationGuide/lifecycle.html#twonode"> two node
49  * replication group</a>}, if the primary goes down, the application may want
50  * the secondary node to continue to service read requests, and will lower the
51  * consistency requirement on that node in order to maintain read availability.
52  *
53  * @see ReplicaConsistencyPolicy
54  * @see <a href="{@docRoot}/../ReplicationGuide/consistency.html"
55  * target="_top">Managing Consistency</a>
56  */
