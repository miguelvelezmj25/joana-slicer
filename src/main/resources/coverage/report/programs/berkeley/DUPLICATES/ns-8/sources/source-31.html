34 /**
35  * Lock statistics for a database environment.
36  *
37  * <p> Note that some of the lock statistics may be expensive to obtain because

40  * com.sleepycat.je.Environment#getLockStats Environment.getLockStats} is
41  * called with a StatsConfig parameter that has been configured for "slow"

44  * @deprecated as of 4.0.10, replaced by {@link
