24 /**
25  * A consistency policy which describes the amount of time the Replica is
26  * allowed to lag the Master. The application can use this policy to ensure
27  * that this node sees all transactions that were committed on the Master
28  * before the lag interval.
29  * <p>
30  * Effective use of this policy requires that the clocks on the Master and
31  * Replica are synchronized by using a protocol like NTP
32  * <p>
33  * Consistency policies are specified at either a per-transaction level through
34  * {@link com.sleepycat.je.TransactionConfig#setConsistencyPolicy} or as an replication node
35  * wide default through {@link
36  * ReplicationConfig#setConsistencyPolicy}
37  *
38  * @see <a href="{@docRoot}/../ReplicationGuide/consistency.html"
39  * target="_top">Managing Consistency</a>
40  */

54     /**
55      * Specifies the amount of time by which the Replica is allowed to lag the
56      * master when initiating a transaction. The Replica ensures that all
57      * transactions that were committed on the Master before this lag interval
58      * are available at the Replica before allowing a transaction to proceed
59      * with Environment.beginTransaction.
60      *
61      * Effective use of this policy requires that the clocks on the Master and
62      * Replica are synchronized by using a protocol like NTP.
63      *
64      * @param permissibleLag the time interval by which the Replica may be out
65      * of date with respect to the Master when a transaction is initiated on
66      * the Replica.
67      *
68      * @param permissibleLagUnit the {@code TimeUnit} for the permissibleLag
69      * parameter.
70      *
71      * @param timeout the amount of time to wait for the consistency to be
72      * reached.
73      *
74      * @param timeoutUnit the {@code TimeUnit} for the timeout parameter.
75      *
76      * @throws IllegalArgumentException if the permissibleLagUnit or
77      * timeoutUnit is null.
78      */
79     public TimeConsistencyPolicy(long permissibleLag,
80                                  TimeUnit permissibleLagUnit,
81                                  long timeout,
82                                  TimeUnit timeoutUnit) {
83         this.permissibleLag = PropUtil.durationToMillis(permissibleLag,
84                                                         permissibleLagUnit);
85         this.timeout = PropUtil.durationToMillis(timeout, timeoutUnit);
86     }
